# Housing Platform

## Affordability Initiatives
- Mandate 15% affordable units in all new developments over 20 units (inclusionary zoning)
- Create a county Housing Trust Fund seeded with $5M from recordation tax surplus
- Expand down payment assistance from $10K to $20K for first-time buyers earning under 120% AMI
- Preserve existing affordable housing stock by funding rehabilitation of 200+ aging units

## Zoning Reform
- Allow accessory dwelling units (ADUs) by-right in all residential zones
- Support transit-oriented density along the MARC corridor (Brunswick, Frederick, Point of Rocks)
- Streamline the development review process — current timeline averages 18 months

## Rental Protections
- Establish a rental licensing and inspection program for health and safety
- Support just-cause eviction protections
- Create a county-funded emergency rental assistance fund

## Key Statistics (verified)
- Median home price in Frederick County: $450K (2025)
- Median household income: $98,000
- Housing cost burden (>30% of income): 34% of renters
- New housing permits 2025: 1,200 (demand estimate: 2,000+)
