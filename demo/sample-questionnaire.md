# Frederick County Apple Ballot Questionnaire — 2026

**Organization**: Frederick County Civic Alliance
**Type**: Apple Ballot / Voter Guide
**Deadline**: April 15, 2026
**Word Limit**: 200 words per response

Please answer the following questions. Your responses will be published in our voter guide distributed to 15,000 Frederick County households.

---

## Question 1: Economic Development
What is your plan for supporting small businesses and economic growth in Frederick County? Please include specific policies or initiatives you would champion.

## Question 2: Education Funding
Frederick County Public Schools face a $45M budget gap. How would you address education funding, and what are your priorities for school spending?

## Question 3: Housing Affordability
Housing costs in Frederick have risen 40% in 5 years. What specific steps would you take to increase housing affordability and availability?

## Question 4: Environmental Protection
How would you balance economic development with environmental protection, particularly regarding the Monocacy River watershed and agricultural land preservation?

## Question 5: Public Safety
What is your approach to public safety, including both law enforcement and community-based safety programs? How would you address the rise in property crime?
