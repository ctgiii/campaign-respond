# Education Platform

## Funding Priorities
- Fully fund the Blueprint for Maryland's Future (Kirwan Commission) at the county level
- Advocate for state funding formula reform — Frederick County sends $0.40 of every tax dollar to Annapolis and gets back $0.22
- Redirect savings from administrative consolidation (estimated $3-5M) into classrooms

## Teacher Retention
- Support competitive teacher salaries — Frederick ranks 8th of 24 MD jurisdictions
- Expand teacher housing assistance program modeled on Montgomery County's program
- Reduce standardized testing burden to give teachers more instructional time

## School Infrastructure
- Prioritize Oakdale Elementary and Lincoln Elementary for renovation (both 50+ years old)
- Invest in HVAC modernization — 14 schools lack adequate air conditioning
- Expand pre-K capacity by 500 seats over 4 years using community facility partnerships

## Key Statistics (verified)
- FCPS enrollment: 46,000 students
- Current per-pupil spending: $15,200 (state avg: $16,800)
- Teacher vacancy rate: 4.2% (2025)
- Blueprint funding gap at county level: estimated $45M
